Our Mission

We aim to promote awareness among our members about HVAC&R, enhance students' technical skills, facilitate interaction among members, and encourage innovation and collaborations with the industry.

Our Activities

Our activities include organizing workshops, seminars, and webinars to educate students about HVAC&R, improve interaction, and foster a sense of community. We also conduct practical training sessions, industry visits, and participate in ISHRAE national competitions to enhance technical skills and promote healthy competition.

Why Join ISHRAE as a Student Member?
Professional Recognition

Gain industry recognition and demonstrate commitment to professionalism and excellence in the HVAC&R industry.

Access to Resources

Get access to technical resources, research papers, and industry standards related to HVAC&R systems.

Competitions

Participate in national level Quiz and Design Competitions, win cash prizes, and build your resume.

Networking

Connect with professionals and industry experts who are willing to share their knowledge and experience.

Professional Development

Benefit from mentoring, leadership training, and opportunities to develop new skills in HVAC&R.

Research Opportunities

Access grants available for conducting research projects in the field of HVAC&R.
